Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I update my ArcadeVerse drivers?

To update your drivers, please visit our downloads page linked in the footer. Select your product model and download the latest driver package. Follow the installation instructions provided in the readme file.

What are the system requirements for Apex Legends Remastered?

Minimum requirements: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 3 1300X, 8GB RAM, NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290. Recommended: Intel Core i7-7700K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600, 16GB RAM, NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 / AMD Radeon RX 5700.

My VR headset isn't detected, what should I do?

First, ensure all cables are securely connected. Try restarting your computer and the VR headset. If the issue persists, check our troubleshooting guide or contact support.
